## THE 6 OBJECTIONS YOU WILL HEAR (and your answers)

Prepare these. Practice saying them out loud.

**1. "I already use [HubSpot / Mailchimp / a CRM]."**
> "Great. Atlantic & Vine isn't a CRM replacement — it's the layer that feeds your CRM. We find the leads, score them, write the outreach. They land in your existing system pre-qualified. Or in our dashboard if you don't have a CRM. Your call."

**2. "How is this different from Apollo?"**
> "Apollo gives you a list. We give you a list, plus the score, plus the audit, plus the first outreach message, plus the social content for your own brand — all from one place. Apollo is a data source. We're the operating system."

**3. "Can I see real results from existing clients?"**
> Honest answer if you're pre-revenue: "We just launched the platform — you'd be one of our first 10 customers. That's why pricing starts at $1,995 instead of $5K. The audit you just got from us IS real results. Want to run another one on a different business of yours?"

**4. "What if it doesn't work?"**
> "Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. The free audit is yours to keep. We only keep you if the platform is making you money."

**5. "How fast can I get started?"**
> "Tomorrow. Fill out the intake form today, you get a magic-link to your dashboard within an hour, first batch of scored leads in your dashboard by end of next business day."

**6. "Can I bring my own existing leads?"**
> "Yes. Upload a CSV and they get deduped against new discoveries, scored, audited automatically. We don't replace your existing data — we make it smarter."
