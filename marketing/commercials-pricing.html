The fine print, but make it human.

What is a commercial, exactly?

For us, a commercial is either a short AI-generated video (6 seconds) or a high-end hero image of your business. Think product shot, lifestyle moment, mood reel, or a story-style clip -- anything you can post on social, drop in an email, or run as an ad.

Do I really get the first one free?

Yes. Take the three-minute audit, we make one commercial of your choice, you download it. No card on file. If it makes you smile, the Client Surge plans are right there.

Why is it bundled into Client Surge tiers instead of sold separately?

Because the commercial is most valuable when it shows up alongside lead generation, scoring, and outreach. Anyone can make an image -- we make the right image for the right prospect at the right moment, plugged into a system that finds and qualifies the lead in the first place.

What if I run out of credits in a busy month?

Top up a la carte: $39 per extra video (10-pack), $9 per extra hero image (20-pack). Or roll up a tier. Unused credits also roll over for 30 days.

Will the commercials look like my brand?

Yes. The audit pulls your colors, your tone, your customer, and your industry into every prompt. The longer you stay on a plan, the smarter your creative profile becomes.

Can I cancel?

Any time, from your portal, no phone call required. We will keep your finished commercials available to download for 90 days after you go.
